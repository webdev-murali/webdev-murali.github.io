How to download images?

  • Mobile: To download images on mobile, long-press on the image and select 'Download.'
  • Desktop or PC: On desktop or PC, hover over the image, right-click with the mouse, and choose 'Save image as...'

How do you get the URL of a YouTube video?

  1. Find the video on  YouTube.
  2. Click the SHARE link on the bottom-right corner of the video.
  3. In the Share a link dialog, click the COPY link to the right of the video URL.
  4. You can now paste the URL in the desired location.
